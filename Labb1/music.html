Underfunk

Underfunk is my music alias. I started using it in 2011 when I recorded som mixtapes and remixes. The name is a combination of "under" and "funk", reflecting my interest in underground and experimental music with an essence of funk sounds.

Listen to it here at Mixcloud.com.

Bio

The beginnig

My journey in music started in 1996 when I began creating playing by chance, because the DJ got fired on the spot, and the owner asked for help. From there, I moved into more music-defined clubs and learning from everyone I met.

The transition

It took some time until I started to take music more seriously and began to produce my own tracks. However, my tracks were not great because I studied and worked in IT and had no time to focus on music. Nevertheless, my dj gigs were good enough to call the attention of key actors in the music industry and I kept playing along with some of my music heroes.

The peak time

My peak time in music was during the middle 2000s when I joined the line-up of the finer clubs in Mallorca. This period was marked by intense creativity and collaboration with international artists.

The decadence

In september 2007 I had a motorcycle accident that changed my life and my approach to music.
